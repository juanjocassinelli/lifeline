# 🧵 Lifeline

An interactive self-reflection exercise to map the key events of your life on a visual timeline.

**Features:**
- Enter your name, date of birth and age to anchor your personal timeline
- Add life events with a description, age and type (Positive, Neutral or Challenging)
- Collision-free labels — events that are close together never overlap
- Colour-coded dots: green for positive, purple for neutral, red for challenging
- Dark and light mode
- English and Spanish
- Download your completed Lifeline as a PDF
